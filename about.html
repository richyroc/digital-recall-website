What do we do?

We are a bunch of video enthusiasts with a couple of DSLRs and a shared concept. We're out to explore big ideas and challenge ourselves creatively.

Why do we do it?

We set ourselves the creative challenge of making a varied collection of videos that approach a topical subject from different angles. We wanted to look at memory in the digital age and some of the big questions that our new technologies present. Where do people store their memories? In their heads, as ever. But all of our laptops, smartphones, external hard drives, social networks, digital cameras and other communication tools do a lot of remembering for us as well.

Each of our videos explore how technology can extend or even change how we remember things, as well as looking good and keeping you entertained, of course!
