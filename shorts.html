Short Movies

Digital Recall

What are the effects of crowd-sourcing our collective memory? This abstract short illustrates the messy collaborations that constitute digital memory archives online (e.g. Wikipedia), asking what is altered or even lost between the edits and re-edits.

Director: Austen Donohoe
Editor: Laura Ni Dhubhghaill
Camera Operators: Melanie Rabier, Laura Ni Dhubhghaill, Cormac O'Brien,
Music: Barie Gledden, Jeff Dale, and Tim Reilly - Room To Shine

Special Thanks to Justin Gotchall, Louise O'Hanlon, Sarah Dolan, Monique Parnis, Ian Finnerty, Derek Cosgrave, Alan Jacob and the Digital Recall Team

Questions of Travel

Short home made video collage, combining old camcorder footage from 1995, with the same family's trip in 2011. Poem by Elizabeth Bishop.

Director and Editor: Laura Ni Dhubhghaill
Music: Dave O'Brien - Beautiful Imperfections

Special Thanks to Damien Hickey, Ian Finnerty, Florence Lourme, Cormac O'Brien, Eileen O'Duill, and J. Alexander Briggs
