A Digital New Hope (For The Socially Inept)

Comedy short about a man who relies on his smartphone to remember the smallest of details. Think you'd be lost without your web devices? There may be (digital) hope for you yet.

Director's Statement

When all the information in the world is in your pocket, what do you really need to remember? As I type, I am within easy reach of four ways to 'update my status'. That includes two games consoles. For someone raised on consoles that only played games, this speaks volumes about the spread of memory-sharing tools. Whatever next?

This short is a response to this proliferation. An absurdist comedy about John, a man who can't even get a name right without his Smartphone. Through John, we present a satirical vision of where we are and, perhaps, where we're headed.

Austen Donohoe
